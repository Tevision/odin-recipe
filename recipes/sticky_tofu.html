Sticky Tofu

Happy Thurday folks take a look at this amazingly healthy and easy to make vegan sticky tofu dish, which will send your taste bud into overdrive

Ingredients

  • 200g Organic Tofu
  • 1 small Cauliflower

For the Sticky Tofu marinade

  • Sprinkling of Sesame Seeds
  • 2 Tbsp.Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp. Tomato Paste
  • 2 Tsp. Vinegar
  • 1 Tsp. Brown Sugar
  • 2 tsp. Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp. Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C and arrange the tofu on a baking tray, covered with baking parchment.
  2. Whisk the marinade ingredients in a bowl and gently coat the tofu pieces evenly.
  3. Bake in the oven for about 20 minutes, turning them halfway and brushing them with the marinade leftovers.
  4. Take out of the oven and while still warm, sprinkle the sesame seeds on top.
  5. In a separate bowl, drizzle the cauliflower florets with the dressing ingredients and mix well.
  6. in a serving bowl, arrange the cauliflower on one side and the tofu on the other.
  7. Sprinkle some fresh chillies and some pesto, if you would like (I had some leftover parsley pesto which was just perfect with the cauliflower).
